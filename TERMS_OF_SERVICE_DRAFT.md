# Atlas Capital Research Terms of Service

Status: Draft for legal review. Not effective and not approved for external
users.

Draft date: June 13, 2026

## Service

Atlas Capital Research is a research, monitoring, reporting, portfolio
analysis, and paper-trading service. Features may be experimental, unavailable,
delayed, incomplete, or changed without notice during testing.

## Eligibility And Accounts

The planned beta is invite-only and intended for adults who can enter a binding
agreement. Users must provide accurate account information, protect their
identity-provider account, use MFA when required, and promptly report suspected
unauthorized access.

Accounts are personal unless an approved organization workspace permits
additional members. Users may access only workspaces and records they are
authorized to use.

## Research, Not Execution

Atlas currently:

- Does not execute real trades.
- Does not connect to brokerage accounts.
- Does not hold customer funds or securities.
- Does not guarantee investment outcomes.
- Requires human approval for financial decisions.

Market data, news, scores, summaries, model outputs, and recommendations may be
inaccurate, delayed, incomplete, or unavailable. Users are responsible for
independent verification and their own financial decisions.

Whether future personalized features constitute regulated investment advice is
an unresolved legal question. External service must not begin until counsel
reviews the business model, compensation, personalization, marketing,
disclosures, and intended trading workflows.

## Acceptable Use

Users must not:

- Access another tenant's data or test access without written authorization.
- Circumvent authentication, authorization, rate limits, or security controls.
- Upload malware or use Atlas to violate law or third-party rights.
- Scrape, redistribute, resell, or publish licensed market or news data unless
  expressly permitted.
- Misrepresent Atlas output as guaranteed, licensed advice, or an executed
  trade.
- Use automated features to create legal or financial commitments outside
  explicitly approved workflows.

## User Content

Users retain rights in content they provide. They grant Atlas the limited right
to host, process, back up, and display that content to operate and secure the
service. Users represent that they have the right to provide the content.

## Third-Party Services And Data

Atlas may depend on identity, hosting, market-data, news, filing, and analytics
providers. Their terms and availability apply. Atlas does not grant rights to
third-party data beyond rights Atlas is authorized to provide.

## Suspension And Termination

Atlas may suspend or disable access to protect the service, investigate abuse,
comply with law, address nonpayment, or enforce these terms. Account closure
and deletion are subject to tenant ownership, security records, retention
rules, backups, disputes, and legal obligations.

## Disclaimers

The service is provided on an experimental, as-available basis to the extent
permitted by law. No warranty is made regarding accuracy, availability,
fitness, security, non-infringement, or investment performance.

## Limitation Of Liability

Any limitation, exclusion, damages cap, indemnity, arbitration, governing-law,
venue, class-action waiver, and consumer-rights language requires attorney
