Simulation workflow

What Simulate fill does

?

After you approve a paper proposal, Simulate fill records that hypothetical purchase in the paper portfolio. It uses the latest available market price and updates simulated holdings only.

1 Atlas proposes 2 You approve 3 Simulate fill records paper holding 4 Atlas tracks performance
No brokerage order is sent. No real money is spent. This only updates Atlas paper tracking.
